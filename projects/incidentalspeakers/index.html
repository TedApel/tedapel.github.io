This ongoing series of sculptures is based on the idea of a loudspeaker as a representation of sound. In these works I’m exploring the idea that the issues and ideas around sound can be explored artistically without sound itself.

I have written about these works in my article Incidental Speakers available here:

Incidental Speakers

Units of Conveyance

Eight self-contained speakers with asynchronous electronics

Schaeffer

Wooden loudspeaker sculpture inspired by Schaeffer's train etude

Speaker[lego]

Loudspeaker built from lego blocks

One and Four Speakers

Loudspeaker as sculpture, painting, object, and photograph

Modes

Three speakers evoking Chion's listening modes

Acoustic Space

Loudspeaker cone extending into a wall

Senses

Five senses depicted through speaker-scale objects

Production Reproduction

Two speakers depicting sound production and reproduction

Speaker [crochet]

Loudspeaker with a crocheted wool cone

Washer Dryer

Two small speakers playing washer and dryer sounds

Response

Loudspeaker reimagined as tissue box

Ink Speaker

Speaker box filled with ink, after Charles Ray
