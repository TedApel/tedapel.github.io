Incremental Instances is a series of live electroacoustic performances in which sounds are generated and realized as independent sources within a space. Each sound is assigned to a specific speaker that is carried and placed during the performance, gradually assembling a distributed sonic configuration. The work unfolds through accumulation rather than composition, producing a temporary field of discrete, coexisting instances whose relationships arise through spatial placement and continued sounding.
