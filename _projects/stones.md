Handmade eurorack module for amplifying the sounds of stones placed on a wood panel. This module is featured on the album [Findings](http://tedapel.bandcamp.com/album/findings).

- 14 HP
- +/-12V 5mA
- Skiff friendly

Stones module with [Steady State Fate MMF](http://www.steadystatefate.com/mmf) filter and [RYO Penta](http://ljunggrenaudio.com/products/sequencing.html#penta) sequencer.
